# Battery Notes

- Average voltage from two endpoints is a compact thermodynamic descriptor, not a replacement for a full voltage-composition curve.
- Volume-change screening is useful for cycling stability triage, but local distortions and anisotropic strain can still matter.
- Migration barriers and endpoint voltages should be discussed together; a good battery candidate generally needs both acceptable thermodynamics and acceptable kinetics.
- Specific-energy proxies are useful for screening, but they are not a substitute for a full cell-level energy-density analysis.
